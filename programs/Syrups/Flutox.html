What it is?

Flutox is a cough suppressant medication for adults and children. It can be taken three times a day to relieve dry cough. In this way, it improves the well-being of people suffering from non-productive, irritating, or nervous cough associated with colds, flu, allergies, etc.

Secondary Effects.

The most common side effects, although rare, are drowsiness and dry mouth. The possible side effects include:

  • Uncommon: drowsiness, dry mouth (at high doses).
  • Very rare: allergic reaction, hives.
