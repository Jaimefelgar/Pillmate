What it is?

Dalsy is a syrup medicine made With ibuprofen, that makes the dalsy an excellent medication for symptoms like low pains and fever. This medicine is specially used for little kids, wich can't eat a pill of ibuprofen and this is a simplier way to eat the medication in a more easy way and adapted to their weight.

Secondary Effects.

Dalsy Secondary effects appear mostly in peaple over 65 years old, some of the secondary effects the usually suffer are:

  • Peptic ulcers.
  • Digestive hemorrhage.
  • Perforation.

    • In most of the cases the secondary effects should be a general disconfort or stomach pain.
