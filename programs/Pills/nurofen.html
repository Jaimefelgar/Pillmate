Nurofen

What it is?

Ibuprofen, the active ingredient in this medicine, works by reducing pain and fever. It is indicated for adults and adolescents over 12 years of age for the symptomatic relief of occasional mild to moderate pain, such as headache, dental pain, menstrual cramps, muscle pain (contractures), or back pain (lumbago), as well as in cases of fever. You should consult a doctor if your condition worsens or does not improve, if the fever lasts more than 3 days, or if the pain persists for more than 3 days in adolescents or 5 days in adults.
