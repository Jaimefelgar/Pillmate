Nolotil

What it is?

Nolotil belongs to the group of medications known as "Other Analgesics and Antipyretics. This medication is used for the treatment of: Moderate or severe acute pain, such as post-operative or post-traumatic pain, colicky pain, or pain of tumoral origin. High fever that does not respond to other measures or other medications for fever.

Secondary Effects.

Common side effects

  • Hypotension (low blood pressure).

    • Uncommon side effects

  • Skin rashes and skin reactions

    • Rare side effects

  • Allergic reactions that typically occur during or just after administration, but may also appear hours later.
  • Rashes and the appearance of hives on the skin.
  • Decrease in the number of white blood cells (leukopenia).
  • Asthma.

    • Dose.

  • Adults and adolescents aged 15 years or older (weighing more than 53 kg) may take 1 capsule (575 mg of metamizole) as a single dose, which can be administered up to 6 times per day, at intervals of 4 to 6 hours. The maximum daily dose is 3,450 mg (corresponding to 6 capsules). The effect of the medication usually begins 30 to 60 minutes after oral administration.
  • Children and adolescents under 15 years of age Nolotil should not be used in children under 15 years of age. For younger children, other presentations and dosages of this medication are available; consult your doctor or pharmacist.
  • Elderly patients and patients in poor general health or with renal impairment The dose should be reduced in elderly patients, debilitated individuals, and those with impaired kidney function, as the elimination of metamizole metabolites may be delayed.
