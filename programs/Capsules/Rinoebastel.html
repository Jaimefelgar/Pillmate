Rino-Ebastel

What it is?

Rino-Ebastel contains a combination of two active ingredients: ebastine and pseudoephedrine. Ebastine is an antihistamine (anti-allergic), and pseudoephedrine is a nasal decongestant. Antihistamines help reduce allergy symptoms by preventing the effects of histamine release. Decongestants help reduce nasal congestion. Rino-Ebastel is indicated for the relief of nasal and ocular symptoms associated with seasonal allergic rhinitis, when accompanied by nasal congestion, in adults and adolescents aged 12 years and older.
