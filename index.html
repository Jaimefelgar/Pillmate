Home

Our group has created this page with the aim of helping sick people and preventing self-medication. We will do this through the following website, where users can select and view the specifications of various medications to serve as a helpful resource for this group of people.

Having said that, we would like to emphasize that we are not experts on the subject, and self-medication without the supervision of a licensed doctor is not recommended at all. With our website, we only aim to prevent anyone from taking medication for something unrelated to their condition.

Another thing we would like to emphasize is that the website is currently just a prototype.
